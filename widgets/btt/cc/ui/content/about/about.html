Breezin’ Thru®

Breezin' Thru makes learning fun and gives students the thrill of creating music. It is the only product line-up that links music theory to composition allowing users to apply the theory concepts they are learning in a meaningful way.

Breezin’ Thru Theory provides you with a fun way to learn music theory concepts and build mastery; while Breezin’ Thru Composing helps you apply what you’ve learned to create your own music. These 2 products are designed to work hand-and-hand as a complete package, but you can also use them separately.

Breezin’ Thru® Composing

Breezin’ Thru Composing is a cool interactive composition lessons app that let’s students discover the thrill of making music on any device …iPad/tablets, computers or smart phones.

  • Fun, ready-to-go lessons & activities for grades 5 to 12
  • Links theory to the thrill of making music
  • Supports curriculum standards
  • Can be used with notation and sequencing software
  • Engages students online at home or at school

How it works

Breezin’ Thru Composing follows our unique Breezing Thru® 5-Step learning module. Each chapter contains:

  1. Interactive Lessons – covering the key compositional concepts
  2. Composition Model – includes step-by-step video
  3. Create your own Composition – uses notation software
  4. Reflect & Discuss – students reflect on their pieces
  5. Expand It – students are inspired to take their work further, making it sound even cooler! Uses tools like GarageBand etc.
